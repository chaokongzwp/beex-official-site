1. Personal Information We Collect and Use

We only collect personal information for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as permitted by applicable law. We may collect the following categories of information while you use BeeX:

  • Identity data: name, surname, gender, profile picture, date of birth, and government-issued identity information where required.
  • Contact data: delivery address, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, TikTok account, Shopee account, social network identifiers, and customer service communication records.
  • Account and payment data: bank account details, debit or credit card details, payment details, payout profiles, expiry dates, and related payment verification information.
  • Transaction data: order details, product lists, affiliate link activity, cashback, commission, wallet, withdrawal, and other service records related to you.
  • Technical data: IP address, login data, browser type, browser version, time zone, device model, operating system, platform, IMEI, MAC address, device identifier, cookies, and similar technologies.
  • Profile and usage data: username, password or authentication data, PIN, OTP data, SSO data, browsing history, search records, usage behavior, preferences, feedback, and survey responses.
  • Marketing and communication data: your communication preferences, marketing preferences, chat history, email history, and call history with BeeX or our service providers.
  • Additional verification data: information required to verify identity, prevent fraud, handle disputes, comply with law, or investigate a suspected violation of BeeX policies.

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To help you create, access, and manage a BeeX account.
  • To provide login services through WhatsApp, phone number, TikTok, Google, Apple, or other authorized third-party accounts.
  • To process product links, generate affiliate sharing links, calculate cashback, synchronize platform orders, settle wallet balances, and support withdrawals.
  • To process orders, provide customer support, respond to inquiries, handle complaints, resolve disputes, and communicate service status.
  • To verify and carry out payment transactions, bank transfers, e-wallet transactions, payout transactions, and related settlement services.
  • To provide marketing information, promotional offers, coupons, campaigns, product recommendations, and affiliate opportunities when allowed by law or consent.
  • To conduct fraud detection, risk control, due diligence, audit, compliance, legal claim handling, and platform security monitoring.
  • To analyze service usage, improve user experience, customize content, conduct research, develop new services, and produce aggregated statistics.

We may collect personal information directly from you, from your use of BeeX services, from customer service interactions, from third-party service providers, from payment or logistics partners, from social login providers, from platform partners, and from other lawful sources.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

BeeX may use cookies, local storage, device identifiers, pixels, software development kits, and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, improve performance, analyze traffic, maintain security, prevent fraud, measure campaigns, and provide more relevant content.

You may manage cookies through browser or device settings. If you block or delete cookies, some BeeX functions may not work correctly, including login, link tracking, cashback attribution, and transaction security.

3. Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

BeeX does not sell your personal information. We may share necessary information only for legitimate business, service, security, legal, and compliance purposes.

  • We may share information with BeeX affiliates to provide account, wallet, affiliate, campaign, customer service, security, and operational services.
  • We may share information with trusted partners such as payment providers, payout providers, cloud service providers, data analytics providers, customer support providers, logistics partners, marketing service providers, fraud prevention providers, and platform partners such as TikTok Shop, Shopee, WhatsApp, and other commerce or communication platforms.
  • We may transfer personal information if BeeX undergoes merger, acquisition, restructuring, bankruptcy, asset transfer, or similar business change, subject to lawful safeguards.
  • We may publicly disclose personal information only when required by law, with your separate consent, or where the information has lawfully become public.

When sharing information, we require recipients to process the information according to this Privacy Policy, our instructions, and applicable law.

4. Protection and Retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, loss, misuse, or destruction. These measures may include encryption, access controls, audit logs, de-identification, risk monitoring, and security reviews.

Personal information collected and generated in Indonesia is generally stored in Indonesia. Where cross-border transfer is necessary for service operation, compliance, security, or partner cooperation, we implement legally required safeguards such as consent, contractual protection, security assessment, or data de-identification.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide BeeX services, fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal retention requirements, resolve disputes, maintain business records, and protect legitimate rights. After the retention period expires, we delete or anonymize personal information unless otherwise required by law.

5. Managing Your Personal Information

We respect your rights to access, correct, supplement, delete, and manage your personal information, subject to applicable legal exceptions.

  • You may view and update account information, profile information, phone number, WhatsApp number, delivery information, and other editable information in BeeX account settings where available.
  • You may view relevant order, wallet, cashback, and withdrawal records through BeeX service pages where available.
  • You may request deletion of personal information if our processing violates law, if we collected information without required consent, if our processing violates our agreement with you, if you cancel your BeeX account, or if we terminate the relevant service.
  • You may change or withdraw authorization by adjusting device permissions, privacy settings, account settings, or by contacting us. Some services may become unavailable after consent is withdrawn.
  • You may request account cancellation. After cancellation, BeeX will stop providing the relevant products and services and delete or anonymize personal information as required by applicable law.

For security, we may ask you to submit a written request or verify your identity before processing requests. We strive to respond within 30 days after receiving and verifying your request. We may refuse requests that are repetitive, excessive, technically impractical, unrelated to your identity, harmful to the rights of others, or restricted by law.

6. Minors

BeeX attaches great importance to protecting minors' personal information. Minors may not create BeeX accounts without consent from a parent or legal guardian. If you are under 18, please use BeeX only with consent and supervision from your parent or guardian.

If we discover that personal information from a minor has been collected without verifiable consent from a parent or guardian, we will make reasonable efforts to delete the relevant information promptly.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in BeeX services, business operations, legal requirements, technology, or security practices. Updated versions will be posted on the BeeX app and official website. For material changes, we may provide more prominent notice through announcements, messages, login prompts, or other appropriate channels.

Material changes may include changes to processing purposes, categories of personal information, methods of use, ownership structure, main sharing recipients, your rights, contact channels, or personal information security risk assessments.
